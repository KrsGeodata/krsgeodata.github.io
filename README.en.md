# Public Sector Research, Innovation and Experimentation

This site serves as the public entry point for Kristiansand municipality's work within research, innovation and digital transformation.

Through collaboration between the municipality, academia, students, public agencies and industry partners, we explore new approaches, technologies and service concepts before major investments and procurements are carried out.

The goal is simple:

> Understand first. Specify second. Procure last.

---

## About the FoU Program

The FoU Program was established to strengthen the municipality's ability to:

- Explore real-world challenges
- Validate needs and hypotheses
- Develop and test MVPs
- Build internal capability
- Reduce procurement risk
- Share lessons learned openly

Areas of focus include:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Planning and Building Services
- Geospatial Information
- Digital Citizen Services
- Data Sharing
- Public Sector Digitalization

## Working Principles

We believe in:

- Open innovation
- Practical experimentation
- Cross-disciplinary collaboration
- Early validation
- Knowledge sharing
- Reuse across organizations

Whenever possible, methods, documentation, lessons learned and code are published openly for others to build upon.
